3 simple steps to start

Step 1

Create your personal account by registering your unique username, and email. Immediately get two unique web links for your new account (1 private, 1 public).

Step 2

Visit your private web link to customize your settings & create/manage your courses & set available slots. Share your public web link via social media, email, or anywhere, to invite learners.

Step 3

Interested learners visit your public web link, see the classes you teach, and book a slot. You get confirmation of the learner's payment, and that's it. Join the class at the appointed time, and teach.
