3 easy steps to start

Step 1

Create your personal account by registering your unique username, email, and skillset. Immediately claim 2 unique URLs for your new account (1 private, 1 public).

Step 2

Visit your private URL to customize your settings & classes window, create classes & set available slots. Share your public URL on social media, or anywhere, to invite learners.

Step 3

Interested learners visit your unique URL, and book for classes by scheduling a slot. Confirm learner's payment & teach via video call. Earn star ratings. Repeat.
